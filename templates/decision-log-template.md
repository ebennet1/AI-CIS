## Usage Guidance

Use this log to prevent re-litigation of prior decisions and to preserve the business context behind each decision.

Each decision should include:

- The decision that was made
- Who owns the decision
- Why the decision was made
- Which teams, systems, or processes are impacted
- Whether the decision is final, proposed, or superseded
- When the decision should be revisited
